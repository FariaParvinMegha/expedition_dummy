Julian Evans

Adventurer, Fundraiser & Speaker

Julian Evans is a British Adventurer, dedicated Fundraiser and Inspirational Speaker. On his quest to conquer the coveted 'Explorer's Grand Slam', Julian has successfully reached both poles and climbed six of the world's seven summits including Mount Everest in 2019. Julian remains dedicated to fundraising for the causes he is passionate about, and has many exciting adventures ahead.
